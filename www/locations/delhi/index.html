About Us

Pyladies Delhi, started in October 2014,is the Delhi chapter of Pyladies, an international mentorship group focused on helping more women become active participants and leaders in the python open-source community. We aim to provide a friendly support network for women and a bridge to the larger Python world. Anyone with an interest in Python is encouraged to participate!

Activities

The mission of PyLadies Delhi is to promote, educate and advance a diverse Python community through outreach, education, conferences, events and social gatherings.
